IBYCUS

and the son of gold-girh Hyllis, aye and he to
whom Troilus for loveliness of form was likened
forthwith both by Trojan and Danaam e'ven as gold
thrice refined might be likened to mountain copper.

Theirs it is to share beauty for ever, and thine,
too, Polycrates, shall be a glory, even as my glory

in song, unfading.
